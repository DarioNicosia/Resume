About Me

I’m an Italian web developer living in Lisbon, Portugal. After having worked for several years in the audiovisual field as a videomaker, driven by a great curiosity I approached the coding's world. I discovered a complex and at the same time fascinating environment. It was like knowing a new language that is becoming more and more familiar day by day. I focused on many different areas of web development from back end programming (Node.js, Express, MongoDB) to front end engineering (HTML, CSS, and jQuery/Javascript/ Vue.js), digital accessibility, user experience and visual design. I’m extremely passionate about web development and design in all it’s forms. Aside from web development, I enjoy spending my time traveling, discovering new spots and cultures, taking photos and playing & watching sports and watching films.
